Cappuccino

Strong foamy espresso based bevie made with equal parts micro-dense foam to hot textured milk. A reg (8oz) cup = 105 mls foam / 105 milk / 30mls espresso.

Latte

Latte - Milky espresso based bevie consisting of a thin to medium layer of micro-dense foam sitting on 80% silky, hot, textured milk. A reg (8oz) = 50ml foam / 160 mls milk / 30mls espresso.

Macchiato

Macchiato - Espresso stained with micro-dense foam and marked by a small amount of milk. A piccolo cup (4oz) = 30mls espresso / 5mls milk / 25mls foam.
